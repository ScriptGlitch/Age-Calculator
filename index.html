Zodiac Zodiac (রাশিচক্র)
The zodiac is an imaginary belt in the sky that extends approximately 8 degrees north or south of the ecliptic, which is the apparent path of the Sun across the sky throughout the year. It is divided into twelve equal parts, each associated with a specific constellation and corresponding to a particular astrological sign. The Twelve Zodiac Signs:
01. Aries (মেষ) (March 21 - April 19)
02. Taurus (বৃষ) (April 20 - May 20)
03. Gemini (মিথুন) (May 21 - June 20)
04. Cancer (কর্কট) (June 21 - July 22)
05. Leo (সিংহ) (July 23 - August 22)
06. Virgo (কন্যা) (August 23 - September 22)
07. Libra (তুলা) (September 23 - October 22)
08. Scorpio (বৃশ্চিক) (October 23 - November 21)
09. Sagittarius (ধনু) (November 22 - December 21)
10. Capricorn (মকর) (December 22 - January 19)
11. Aquarius (কুম্ভ) (January 20 - February 18)
12. Pisces (মীন) (February 19 - March 20)
In astrology, the position of celestial bodies, including the Sun, Moon, and planets, within these signs at the time of a person's birth is believed to influence their personality, behavior, and life events. Many people look to their zodiac signs for insights into their characteristics, compatibility with others, and predictions for the future.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the Age Calculator?
To use the Age Calculator, simply input your birth date and the current date. The tool will automatically calculate your age and present the results in a clear and organized format.
Can I calculate my age for a specific date in the past or future?
Yes, the Age Calculator allows you to specify any date, past or future, to determine your age at that particular time.
What additional information does the Age Calculator provide?
In addition to calculating your age, the tool offers fascinating facts about your life, including total months lived, significant milestones, zodiac sign information, and comparisons of your age in animal years and on other planets.
Is the Age Calculator free to use?
Yes, the Age Calculator is completely free to use and accessible to anyone with an internet connection.
Is my personal information safe when using the Age Calculator?
Absolutely! The Age Calculator does not require any personal information to function, ensuring your privacy and security while using the tool.
Can I share my results?
Yes, the Age Calculator provides options to share your results on various social media platforms, allowing you to share your age insights with friends and family.
